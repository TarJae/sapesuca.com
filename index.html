SAPESUCA

Salzburg Peritoneal Surface Calculator
Individualized peritoneal surface area calculation for CRS and HIPEC research.

Open the SAPESUCA App Read the publication

SAPESUCA is an academic, freely accessible and peer-reviewed tool for individualized calculation of the peritoneal surface area. It was developed to support standardized assessment of peritoneal surface area in patients undergoing cytoreductive surgery and HIPEC-related research.

Individualized Patient-specific estimation of peritoneal surface area rather than simplified assumptions.
Research-focused Supports structured assessment in CRS, HIPEC, and peritoneal metastasis research.
Open access Provided free of charge for scientific and professional clinical use.

Why this matters

The tool may help quantify patient-specific and resected peritoneal surface area in the context of cytoreductive surgery. This is clinically relevant because intraperitoneal treatment concepts often rely on simplified assumptions rather than individualized surface area estimates.

SAPESUCA may support future research into more personalized CRS and HIPEC workflows, including dose-related research questions and the standardized comparison of cytoreduction across institutions.

Professional use and disclaimer: SAPESUCA is intended for professional education, scientific use, and clinical research support. It does not replace clinical judgment, validated institutional protocols, or individual medical advice.
